Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Bingo Multiplayer ("the App", "we", "us") is a free browser-based multiplayer bingo game. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you play, how it is used, and what choices you have. We aim to collect only what is necessary to make the game work.

1. What We Collect

a) Information you provide

  • Display name — the name you enter to play (e.g. "Lucky Panda" or your chosen nickname). Guest names are not verified or associated with a real identity.
  • Email address — only if you choose to register with email/password or Google Sign-In.
  • Google account info — if you use "Sign in with Google", we receive your Google display name, email address, and profile picture from Google.

b) Automatically collected technical data

  • IP address — collected automatically by Firebase/Google when you connect to the game server. We use this only for security (rate limiting, abuse prevention). We do not store it ourselves, but Google may log it per their own policies.
  • Approximate location — inferred from your IP address by Firebase infrastructure. We do not request precise GPS location; we do not use location for any purpose in the game.
  • Device and browser type — your browser sends a User-Agent string that tells us the type of device (phone/tablet/desktop) and browser you are using. This is used to optimise the game display and diagnose bugs.
  • Usage timestamps — when rooms are created, when numbers are called, when games end. Used to manage active sessions and clean up stale rooms.

c) Game data

  • Room codes, game mode and win-pattern settings you choose
  • Your bingo card (randomly generated numbers)
  • Numbers you have marked on your card
  • Whether you have achieved Bingo
  • Turn order and caller history within a game

d) Anonymous user identifiers

If you play as a guest, Firebase Authentication generates a random anonymous UID for your session. This ID is not linked to any real-world identity. It persists in your browser's localStorage for the duration of your session.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. The App stores the following in your browser's localStorage (not cookies):

bingo_highlight Saves your "highlight called numbers" preference (on/off). Purely cosmetic.
firebase:authUser:... Firebase stores your authentication session token so you stay logged in across page refreshes. This is set by the Firebase SDK and is required for the app to function.

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain (Settings → Privacy → Site Data → Clear).

We do not use browser cookies for tracking. Firebase may set cookies for security purposes; see Google Firebase Privacy.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To run the game — sync your bingo card, called numbers, and player list in real time across all players in a room.
  • To display your name — your chosen display name is shown to other players in the same room only.
  • Security and abuse prevention — IP and session data help us block bad actors and prevent cheating.
  • Debugging — device/browser info helps us fix compatibility issues.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share it with any third party other than our hosting provider (Firebase/Google, described below).

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

All game data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database hosted by Google. Firebase Authentication manages your session. Data may reside in Google Cloud data centres in the United States and/or Europe.

By using the App, you consent to your data being transferred to and processed by Google's infrastructure. For full details see Firebase Privacy Policy and Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

  • Guest sessions — anonymous auth data in localStorage is cleared when you clear your browser data. The corresponding Firebase anonymous UID may persist in Firebase until purged during routine maintenance.
  • Game rooms — finished or abandoned rooms are not automatically deleted but contain no personal information beyond your chosen display name.
  • Registered accounts — email and display name are retained until you request deletion.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of data associated with your account. To exercise these rights, please contact us (see below). For guests, clearing your browser's site data effectively removes all locally held data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
